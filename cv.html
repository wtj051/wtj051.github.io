Public CV recommendation: use a U.S.-style academic CV for the website. Do not include date of birth, nationality, private phone numbers, home address, or a CV photo unless a specific application requires them.

Public CV checklist

  • Name, current appointment, institutional affiliation, and professional email.
  • Research areas: power systems, smart grids, battery intelligence, energy storage, data-center energy systems, and trustworthy AI for critical infrastructure.
  • Methods: physics-guided machine learning, optimization, reinforcement learning, graph/time-series learning, uncertainty quantification, and digital-twin simulation.
  • Appointments and education.
  • Selected publications and complete publication list.
  • Funded projects, resource allocations, software, datasets, benchmarks, and reproducible research artifacts.
  • Teaching, mentoring, service, honors, and technical skills.
