Faculty-market positioning. I am building an independent research program on trustworthy, physics-guided AI for grid-battery-data-center systems, with public benchmark infrastructure for reproducible evaluation, safe decision support, and student-centered training.

Research agenda

Safe grid operation

Physics-guided learning, graph/time-series models, reinforcement learning, and optimization for power-flow feasibility, voltage/security constraints, emergency control, and resilient operation under uncertainty.

Life-aware battery intelligence

SOH/SOC/RUL estimation, future-capacity prediction, degradation-aware dispatch, and health-to-value decision tools for grid-edge storage, EVs, data centers, and battery systems.

Data-center energy intelligence

Learning-based modeling and optimization for flexible AI-driven data-center demand, storage planning, renewable integration, grid-aware workload flexibility, and multi-timescale coordination under uncertainty.

Featured public benchmark

TrustEnergyBench

TrustEnergyBench is a public benchmark for evaluating AI agents and foundation-model components in critical energy systems. It combines public/synthetic grid scenarios, public battery degradation datasets, and data-center flexibility scenarios to test physical feasibility, robustness, uncertainty awareness, and system-level value.
